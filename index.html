Keming Shen 沈可铭

I am a second-year M.Sc. student in the Computer Vision Institute (CVI) @ Shenzhen University (SZU), supervised by Prof. Linlin Shen. Previously, I received my B.E. degree in Software Engineering (Tencent Cloud Artificial Intelligence Specialized) from the College of Computer Science and Software Engineering (CSSE) at SZU in 2024.

My research interests mainly revolve around Computer Vision, 3D Human Motion Generation, Embodied AI and Reinforcement Learning. I am actively seeking (1) Ph.D. positions for Fall 2027 and (2) other research collaborations related to Human Modeling or AIGC tasks. Feel free to reach out via my email: 2400101005@mails.szu.edu.cn or WeChat: skm1192405754. Always open to discussions, collaborations, or a virtual coffee.

I enjoy drawing🖌️, playing the electronic piano🎹, table tennis🏓, and swimming🏊. In addition, I am also interested in basketball🏀, photography📷, cooking🍝, and reading📖.
