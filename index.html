Hi there👋👋, I am a second-year M.Sc. student at the Computer Vision Institute (CVI) @ Shenzhen University (SZU), where I am advised by Prof. Linlin Shen. Previously, I received my B.E. in Software Engineering (Tencent Cloud Artificial Intelligence Specialized) from the College of Computer Science and Software Engineering (CSSE) @ SZU in 2024.

I am passionate about exploring the principles behind advanced technologies and developing a deeper understanding of the fundamental laws that govern them. My research interests mainly revolve around Computer Vision, 3D Human Motion Generation, Embodied AI, and Reinforcement Learning. I am actively seeking (1) Ph.D. positions for Fall 2027 and (2) research collaborations related to Human Modeling or AIGC. Feel free to reach out via email at 2400101005@mails.szu.edu.cn or on WeChat at skm1192405754. I am always open to discussions, collaborations, or a virtual coffee.

I also have a wide range of interests. I enjoy drawing🖌️, playing the electronic piano🎹, table tennis🏓, and swimming🏊. My artwork received the Bronze Prize in the 2016 "Tianyan International Drawing Competition", and I hold a Level 10 certificate in electronic piano. In addition, I am interested in basketball🏀, photography📷, cooking🍝, and reading📖.
